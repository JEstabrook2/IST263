Frye Island is situated in the middle of Sebago Lake, which is one of Maine's largest lakes. The island is roughly 1.5 miles wide and 2.5 miles long.Access to the island is primarily via a ferry service from the town of Raymond. Once the lake freezes or the ferry service ends for the season, the island is generally inaccessible. Frye Island offers a variety of recreational activities, predominantly centered around lake life, including boating, fishing, swimming, and water sports.The island features a nine-hole golf course, several beaches, tennis courts, and a community center that hosts various activities during the summer months.The island is known for its scenic beauty, surrounded by the clear waters of Sebago Lake and featuring a mix of wooded areas and 13 beaches to enjoy waterfront views.
